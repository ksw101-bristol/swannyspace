PROGRAMME PRINCIPLES

📈
Progressive overload: Add weight or a rep each session. Log every lift — this is the primary driver of hypertrophy.
🎯
RPE-based effort: RPE 7 = 3 reps left in tank. RPE 8 = 2 left. RPE 9 = 1 left. Week 7 pushes to RPE 9.
⏱️
Rest periods: Compounds (bench, row, squat, press) 2–3 min. Isolation work 60–90 sec.
🔁
Two-week phases: Weeks repeat exercises so you can load heavier in week 2 of each phase, then exercises rotate for the next phase.
🍗
Nutrition: 0.8–1g protein per lb bodyweight. A 200–300 kcal surplus maximises muscle building across a 7-week block.
😴
Sleep: 7–9 hours. Most muscle protein synthesis happens at rest — especially in Weeks 5–7.
